Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request copies of your personal data and information about how we process it.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

Right to Portability

Request transfer of your data to another service provider.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data during AI memory editing and video processing:

  • End-to-end encryption for all video uploads and processing
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular data backups and disaster recovery procedures
  • Compliance with industry-standard security frameworks
